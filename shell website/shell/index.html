ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Matt Tomich. I am a Front-End Web Developer from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. I am a motivated self-starting individual with an extreme passion to be creative. I enjoy learning new things and the challenges that come with learning them. I am constantly researching the latest technologies to make websites more modern, clean, organized, and unique while providing a smooth yet dynamic user experience.


I am currently pursuing an Associate in Science Degree in Multimedia Technologies with a concentration in Web and Interactive Design from Pittsburgh Technical Institute. I have a strong knowledge of HTML5, CSS, CSS3, and Javascript, and I am very comfortable with JQuery, PHP, MySQL and Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, Audition, Premiere, and After Effects. This portfolio website will showcase my abilities with responsive web design, and both front-end and back-end web development.
